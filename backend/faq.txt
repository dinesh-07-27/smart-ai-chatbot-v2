How do I reset my password?:To reset your password, go to Settings → Account → Reset Password. You'll receive an email with a reset link. If you don't receive an email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder.
What payment methods do you accept?:We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex) and UPI/NetBanking for payments in India.
How do I track my order?:Open Orders → Select order → Track. You can also provide your order number and we will fetch the latest status.
Can I change my shipping address after ordering?:Address changes are possible within 2 hours of placing the order. Contact support with your order ID for faster assistance.
How do refunds work?:Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after approval.
How do I cancel my order?:You can cancel orders within 1 hour of placing them from the Orders page. If the order is already shipped, contact support for return instructions.
What is the return policy for damaged items?:If your item arrives damaged, contact support within 7 days with photos and order ID. We will initiate return or replacement and process refunds after inspection.
How do I change my account email?:Go to Settings → Account → Email → Change Email. We will send a confirmation link to the new email to verify the change.
How do I change my phone number?:In Settings → Account → Phone, enter the new number and verify with the OTP sent to that number.
How long does shipping take?:Standard shipping: 5–7 business days. Express shipping: 1–3 business days (availability depends on location).
How can I get a tax invoice?:Invoices are available under Orders → Invoice. You can download PDF invoices for completed purchases.
What should I do if I forgot my login details?:Use 'Forgot Password' on the login page to receive reset instructions by email. If you can't access the registered email, contact support with order proof.
How do subscription billing cycles work?:Subscriptions auto-renew on the chosen frequency. You will be charged at the start of each billing period; cancel anytime before renewal to avoid charges.
How can I update my billing information?:Update cards or bank details under Account → Billing. Changes take effect on the next billing cycle.
Do you ship internationally?:We currently ship to selected countries. Check the shipping section at checkout for available countries and rates.
How do I apply a promo code?:Enter the promo code at checkout in the 'Apply Promo' field. Only one promo code can be applied per order unless stated otherwise.
How can I contact customer support?:You can contact support via Support → Contact Us (email), or call our helpline during business hours. Provide your order ID for faster assistance.
What is the warranty on products?:Warranty varies by product. Check the product page for warranty details; for warranty claims, contact support with proof of purchase.
Can I change or cancel a subscription?:Yes — go to Subscriptions in your account to pause, change, or cancel. Changes apply from the next billing cycle.
How do I return an item for exchange?:Open a return request in Orders, choose 'Exchange', ship the item per instructions, and we will dispatch the replacement after receiving the original.
How do I report fraudulent charges?:Contact support immediately with transaction details. We will investigate and liaise with payment providers for chargebacks if necessary.
How is my personal data used?:We use personal data to process orders and improve services. See our Privacy Policy for details on storage, sharing, and deletion requests.
How do I delete my account?:Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will remove personal data per our retention policy; some records (invoices) are retained for legal reasons.
What if I receive the wrong item?:Contact support with photos and order ID within 7 days. We'll arrange pickup and either replace or refund the order as appropriate.
Can I request expedited processing for returns?:Yes — contact support with order ID and reason; expedited returns may have a fee depending on service selected.
How do I leave feedback or rate a product?:After delivery, go to Orders → Rate Product or visit the product page to submit a review and rating.
